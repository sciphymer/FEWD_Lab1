Kangeroo

The word "kangaroo" derives from the Guugu Yimithirr word gangurru, referring to grey kangaroos.

  • Genus: Macropus
  • Average Length: 2 meters tall
  • Average Lifespan: 6 years
  • Habitat: Australia open wood land

Kangaroos have large, powerful hind legs, large feet adapted for leaping, a long muscular tail for balance, and a small head.
